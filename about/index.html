I am an environmental scientist at the Paul Scherrer Institute (PSI) in Switzerland. My research focuses on understanding the sources, transformation, and impacts of air pollution by integrating advanced analytical chemistry and numberical modeling.

Currently, I develop and apply high-resolution mass spectrometry techniques to investigate the molecular composition of organic aerosols, and combine these observations with regional chemical transport models such as GEOS-Chem as well as statistic models to assess pollution sources and associated health risks in regions including South Asia and Africa.
